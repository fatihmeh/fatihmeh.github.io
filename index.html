Hi, This is Fatih!

I am a computer-aided design (CAD) specialist with programmer skills and an aircraft maintenance planning coordinator. I have managed to become a SolidWorks Champion and a Certified SolidWorks Expert (CSWE).


My professional years started in 2009 as a 3D designer and drafter at a concrete batching plant manufacturer. Continued at a telecommunication tower manufacturer. After that, leaned towards aviation to work with a design organization that creates various types of projects for flying vehicles. Since 2019 January I had the title of an aircraft maintenance planning coordinator that lasted up to 2024 August.


I am fluent in design and drafting standards (ASME, ISO), design and production organization regulations (Part 21.J & G), aircraft maintenance (Part-145) and airworthiness procedures (Part-M). I have learned how to develop macros, design methods to automate processes.


You can access my GitHub, GitLab, and LinkedIn profiles.
